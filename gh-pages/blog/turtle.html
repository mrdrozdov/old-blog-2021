December 11, 2018

Congrats! You've invented a brilliant idea for understanding language, and now you want to share it in the world. Your idea relies on a "structural representation" of text. Structural representation. Wat.

Okay. Let's try this again. You've written a machine learning model that uses parse trees. What's a parse tree? Glad that you asked. There's many ways to organize your sentence with syntactic or semantic parses.
